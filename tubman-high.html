Brief History of William V.S. Tubman High School

William V.S. Tubman High School was built & equipped through a joint partnership of the Government of Liberia and the San Francisco State College of the United States of America in 1967. The school was first called Monrovia Jr. High School but later renamed in honor of the 18th President of Liberia, Dr. William VS Tubman during the first graduation ceremony of the school in 1968. He was one of the best presidents of Liberia who served from his election in 1944 till his death in 1971.
